The History of Civilization

Ancient History

Ancient history includes the earliest civilizations, such as Mesopotamia, Ancient Egypt, and the Indus Valley Civilization. These cultures laid the foundation for written language, agriculture, and governance.

Medieval History

The medieval period, often called the Middle Ages, spanned from the 5th to the late 15th century. It was characterized by feudal societies, the spread of religions, and the development of art and architecture like Gothic cathedrals.

Modern History

Modern history begins in the late 15th century with events such as the Renaissance, the Age of Exploration, and the Industrial Revolution. This period has seen rapid technological advancement and global interconnectedness.
